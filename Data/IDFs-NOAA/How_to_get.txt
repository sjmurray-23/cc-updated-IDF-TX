NOAA NCEI ATLAS 14 Precipitation Frequency Estimates
Precipitation Frequency Data Server:
https://hdsc.nws.noaa.gov/pfds/index.html

Data accessed from this database is used to calculate the Change Factors

Downloadable data in a raster GIS compatible format is accessible online via https://hdsc.nws.noaa.gov/pfds/pfds_gis.html
For this project the following were accessed:
Volume: 11. Texas
Type: Precipitation frequency estimates; Upper confidence limits; Lower confidence limits
Series: Annual maximum series
Average recurrence interval: 10-year; 100-year
Duration: 6-hour; 12-hour; 24-hour
